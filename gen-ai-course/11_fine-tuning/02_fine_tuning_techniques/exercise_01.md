# Exercise: Comparing Fine-tuning Techniques

## Objective

Compare different fine-tuning techniques and determine which is best for given scenarios.

## Requirements

For each scenario, recommend the best fine-tuning technique and explain your reasoning:

### Scenario 1
- GPU: Single RTX 4090 (24GB)
- Model: 7B parameters
- Goal: Fine-tune for customer service chatbot
- Timeline: 2 weeks

### Scenario 2
- GPU: 8x A100 GPUs (80GB each)
- Model: 70B parameters
- Goal: Maximum quality for legal domain
- Timeline: 1 month

### Scenario 3
- GPU: MacBook M2 (16GB unified)
- Model: 7B parameters
- Goal: Quick experiments with different styles
- Timeline: 1 week

### Scenario 4
- GPU: Single T4 GPU (16GB)
- Model: 13B parameters
- Goal: Need to preserve base model capabilities
- Timeline: 3 weeks

## Time Duration

20 minutes
