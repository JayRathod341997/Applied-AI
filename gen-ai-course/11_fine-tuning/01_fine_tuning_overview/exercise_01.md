# Exercise: Analyzing Fine-tuning Use Cases

## Objective

Analyze different scenarios to determine whether fine-tuning, RAG, or a hybrid approach is best.

## Requirements

For each scenario provided:
1. Identify the best approach (fine-tuning, RAG, or hybrid)
2. Explain your reasoning
3. Consider factors: data availability, latency, cost, quality needs

## Scenarios

### Scenario 1
A company wants to build a legal document assistant that:
- Understands legal terminology
- Can summarize contracts
- Needs to cite specific laws and regulations
- Must reference the latest legal changes

### Scenario 2
An e-commerce company wants a customer service chatbot that:
- Follows specific response guidelines
- Uses consistent tone and style
- Handles common questions
- Needs access to product catalogs

### Scenario 3
A healthcare startup wants a medical Q&A system that:
- Understands medical terminology
- Must be accurate and reliable
- Needs to reference latest research
- Must comply with HIPAA

### Scenario 4
A financial services firm wants a report generator that:
- Produces consistent formatted reports
- Uses specific financial metrics
- Understands industry jargon
- Works offline for security

## Time Duration

30 minutes
