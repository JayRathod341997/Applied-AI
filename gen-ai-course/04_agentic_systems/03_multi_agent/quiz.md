# Quiz: Multi-Agent Systems

## Questions

### Question 1
What is a key benefit of multi-agent systems?

A) Simplicity
B) Specialization
C) Lower cost
D) Simpler debugging

### Question 2
Which pattern has a central coordinator?

A) Peer-to-peer
B) Hierarchical
C) Mesh
D) Ring

### Question 3
What is task decomposition?

A) Breaking complex tasks into smaller subtasks
B) Combining multiple tasks
C) Prioritizing tasks
D) Scheduling tasks

## Answers

1. B) Specialization
2. B) Hierarchical
3. A) Breaking complex tasks into smaller subtasks
