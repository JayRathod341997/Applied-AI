# Quiz: Introduction to Agentic AI

## Questions

### Question 1
What are the three foundational capabilities of Agentic AI?

A) Generation, Classification, Clustering
B) Autonomy, Reasoning, Action
C) Perception, Memory, Output
D) Planning, Execution, Monitoring

### Question 2
Which component of Agentic AI is responsible for gathering information from the environment?

A) Goals
B) Perception
C) Reasoning
D) Feedback

### Question 3
What distinguishes Agentic AI from traditional prompt-based AI?

A) Agentic AI uses larger models
B) Agentic AI maintains state and can take autonomous actions
C) Agentic AI is faster
D) Agentic AI is more expensive

### Question 4
Which of the following is NOT a component of Agentic AI?

A) Goals
B) Perception
C) Training
D) Memory

### Question 5
What level of autonomy means the agent operates with minimal human oversight?

A) Rule-based
B) Guided
C) Supervised
D) Fully Autonomous

### Question 6
In the agent loop, what comes after "Reasoning"?

A) Perception
B) Feedback
C) Act
D) Goal

### Question 7
What is the primary purpose of the "Feedback" component in Agentic AI?

A) To generate text responses
B) To monitor action outcomes and trigger re-planning when needed
C) To store past interactions
D) To make decisions

### Question 8
Which component helps the agent remember past interactions?

A) Goals
B) Plan
C) Memory
D) Action

### Question 9
What type of reasoning involves breaking down complex problems into steps?

A) Chain-of-thought
B) Cause-effect analysis
C) Logical inference
D) Abstraction

### Question 10
In the example of a customer support scenario, what advantage does Agentic AI have over traditional prompts?

A) It uses more memory
B) It can handle multi-step conversations and offer related actions
C) It's faster
D) It's cheaper

## Answers

1. B) Autonomy, Reasoning, Action
2. B) Perception
3. B) Agentic AI maintains state and can take autonomous actions
4. C) Training
5. D) Fully Autonomous
6. C) Act
7. B) To monitor action outcomes and trigger re-planning when needed
8. C) Memory
9. A) Chain-of-thought
10. B) It can handle multi-step conversations and offer related actions
